The Best Movies According to Karan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Home Alone 2: Lost in New York

This is my favourite holiday film. The comedic adventures of Kevin McCallister in the Big Apple are both heartwarming and hilarious.

Kedarnath

A poignant love story set against the backdrop of the devastating 2013 Uttarakhand floods, Kedarnath combines romance and tragedy in a way that leaves a lasting impact. The breathtaking visuals and emotional narrative make this film a personal favorite.

Dead Poets Society

This timeless classic explores the transformative power of education and inspiration. The story of students at Welton Academy and their charismatic English teacher, John Keating, is both thought-provoking and emotionally stirring. Dead Poets Society is a cinematic masterpiece that resonates with me on many levels.
